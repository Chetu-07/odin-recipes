Chole Bhature

Chole Bhature is a popular North Indian dish consisting of spicy chickpea curry (chole) served with deep-fried fluffy bread (bhature).

Paneer Masala

Paneer Masala is a rich and creamy Indian curry made with paneer (Indian cottage cheese) cooked in a spiced tomato-based gravy.

Rajma Chawal

Rajma Chawal is a comforting and nutritious dish made with red kidney beans cooked in a flavorful gravy, served with steamed rice.
