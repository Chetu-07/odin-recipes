Paneer Masala Recipe


Paneer Masala is a rich and creamy North Indian curry made with paneer (Indian cottage cheese) cooked in a flavorful tomato-based gravy.

Ingredients

For Paneer Masala

  • 200g paneer (cut into cubes)
  • 2 tablespoons oil or butter
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 green chili (chopped, optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream or fresh cream
  • 1/2 cup water (adjust as needed)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish




Instructions

Preparing Paneer Masala

  1. Heat oil or butter in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  2. Add chopped onions and sautÃ© until golden brown.
  3. Stir in ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute until fragrant.
  4. Add tomato puree and green chili. Cook until oil separates from the mixture.
  5. Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, and salt. Cook for 2 minutes.
  6. Pour in water and let it simmer for 5 minutes.
  7. Add paneer cubes and mix gently, ensuring they are coated well in the masala.
  8. Stir in garam masala and crushed kasuri methi.
  9. Lower the heat and add cream, stirring well to get a rich, creamy texture.
  10. Simmer for another 2-3 minutes and turn off the heat.
  11. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot.
